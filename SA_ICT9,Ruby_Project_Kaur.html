Fire is Deadly! and the most dangerous fire come from Grease fires and cooking fires which is available at our homes.Since fire can spread quickly they can be very difficult to manage, NFPA reports that close to 30% of fires start in homes, and because of this we have the March Prevention Month. March happened to be Fire Prevention Month, considering that the month showed the highest record of fire incidents nationwide.we are reminded of the hazards and dangers of fire and are encouraged to promote safety consciousness as a positive preventive approach. Im am presenting some simple steps to help prevent fire and keep you safe.
When cooking, keep towels, pot holders and curtains away from flames.
Keep matches, lighters and other ignitable substances in a secured location out of the reach of children, and only use lighters with child-resistant features.
